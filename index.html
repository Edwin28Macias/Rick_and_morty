Rick Sanchez is the exact definition of "mad scientist." He's an alcoholic, he's a genius, he's irresponsible and he's crazy. Rick has just moved into his daughter's Beth's house and there he remembers that he has a grandson named Morty. Without asking anyone, he decides he's going to make him go on all sorts of adventures so that the boy becomes smart like him and doesn't become an idiot like Jerry, Morty's father and Rick's son-in-law.
